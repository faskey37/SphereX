Cloud Services FAQs

Find answers to common questions about our cloud solutions and services.

What cloud platforms do you support?

We support all major cloud platforms including Amazon Web Services (AWS), Microsoft Azure, Google Cloud Platform (GCP), IBM Cloud, and Oracle Cloud. Our team is certified across all these platforms and can help you choose the right one for your specific needs or implement a multi-cloud strategy.

We also provide expertise in niche platforms for specialized workloads when needed.

How do you ensure the security of my cloud environment?

We implement a comprehensive security framework that includes:

  • Network security with firewalls and VPC configurations
  • Identity and access management with MFA and least-privilege principles
  • Data encryption at rest and in transit
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Compliance with industry standards like ISO 27001, SOC 2, GDPR, and HIPAA

Our security team continuously monitors for threats and vulnerabilities.

What's included in your managed cloud services?

Our managed cloud services include:

  • 24/7 monitoring and alerting
  • Performance optimization and tuning
  • Cost management and optimization
  • Security patching and updates
  • Backup management and disaster recovery
  • Incident response and troubleshooting
  • Technical support and consulting

We essentially act as an extension of your IT team to handle all aspects of your cloud infrastructure.

How long does cloud migration typically take?

Migration timelines vary depending on the complexity of your environment, but most migrations take between 4-12 weeks. We follow a structured approach:

  1. Assessment and planning (1-2 weeks): Evaluate your current environment and plan the migration strategy.
  2. Proof of concept (2-3 weeks): Test the migration with a small subset of workloads.
  3. Migration (2-6 weeks): Move your applications and data to the cloud.
  4. Optimization (1-2 weeks): Fine-tune the environment for performance and cost.

We'll provide a detailed timeline after our initial assessment.

Can you help reduce our current cloud costs?

Absolutely. Our cloud cost optimization service typically reduces client cloud spending by 30-50% through:

  • Right-sizing instances to match workload requirements
  • Implementing auto-scaling to match demand
  • Leveraging reserved instances and savings plans
  • Optimizing storage strategies and data lifecycle policies
  • Identifying and eliminating unused resources
  • Implementing tagging for cost allocation

Many clients see a return on investment within the first month of implementing our recommendations.
