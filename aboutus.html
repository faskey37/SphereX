Our Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming a trusted digital transformation partner

Building the Future, One Innovation at a Time

Founded in 2015 by a team of visionary technologists, SphereX began as a small consultancy with a bold mission: to help businesses thrive in the digital age. What started as a boutique firm has grown into a full-service digital transformation powerhouse serving clients across multiple industries and continents.

Our growth has been fueled by a relentless focus on delivering real business value through technology. We've evolved from implementing point solutions to orchestrating comprehensive digital transformations that redefine how organizations operate and compete.

Headquartered in Nagpur with strategic offices in Mumbai and Bangalore, our team of 200+ professionals combines deep technical expertise with industry knowledge to drive meaningful change for our clients.
